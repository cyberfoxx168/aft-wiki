After The Flash: Wintertide is a multiplayer post-apocalyptic roleplaying game created by ChadTheCreator and Flashstorm Interactive, the eighth main game and twelfth installment in the After the Flash series and the first game in the updated timeline - which features new lore, factions, and technology - and the successor of After the Flash: Mirage.

The game takes place on the Sunrise Islands, a fictional archipelago located slightly north of mainland Ohio, during the decade of the 2160s. The game began in the year 2161, 90 years after the bombs fell. The story of the game focuses on the conflicts, life, war, and events of the Sunrise Islands.

The Flash

The years before the Flash were mostly peaceful. Many of the historical points in time before the Flash are unknown by your everyday wastelander these days, however certain key elements remain known. In the United States, Land was annexed and put into the Union; there was an Atlantic Union trade zone; Africa had an economic boom; humanoid AI had been developed; space elevators were built, etc. The US was largely isolationist and peaceful before a dirty nuke was detonated in San Francisco, which drove the US into a vengeful imperialist frenzy in the 2060s, culminating in an apocalyptic nuclear war in the year 2071. Most in hindsight say it was World War 3. Some older folks say it started in Africa; some say it started after the economic collapse in East Asia; most just have conspiracy theories and lay blame on every population not including their own.

Locations

Sunrise City

A once proud space engineering city along the coast of Lake Erie, Sunrise City now lies in a dormant state of ruin. The towers which once stood tall against the clouds are now decrepit, and the roads are cracking and packed with overgrowth. The original residents of the city are gone, being replaced with scavengers hoping to find something of use, bandits prowling the streets, the patrols of numerous factions, and Mutants which creep in the shadows.

Alumni Fortress

The members of the Alumni have settled in the ruined campus of the once-proud Erie Institute of Technology. The Fortress is a rather sprawling settlement, with the once-ruins of buildings now settled by the Alumni and transformed into something of their use. The Alumni let anyone in, but on the condition that they have good or neutral relations with the faction and that they don't enter any of the private buildings. The Fortress is well-known for the enigmatic Compendium tower, which supposedly houses the Alumni's technological research.

Sunrise Maker Factory

A joint 3D-Printing factory and port that has fallen into disarray. The location contains the factory itself, a cluster of buildings and warehouses with abandoned technology and the never-finished shapes of 3D-printed objects. The port is covered with shipping containers and the husks of old machinery, and a settlement comprised of fishermen has been created along one of the docks; a lively community in a dark time.

Bargetown

A scrap city which is a part of the Coalition, Bargetown is considered an island-wide safe zone. Situated on a barge which can move at will, Bargetown is a dense, cramped scrap city known for its food, stores, crime, and amenities. The settlement was formerly controlled by the Winthrop Dynasty, before being ousted in a coup. The lake around Bargetown has been frozen with ice, and it is not known if this inhbits the Barge's movement.

URSF Outpost

Located in a pre-flash monorail station, the USRF took control of the location, and transformed it into a small outpost. The Outpost is surrounded with various defensive measures, from barbed wire to security cameras. As of the USRF claiming of Black Mountain, the frontier outpost has been abandoned by the USRF.

The Quarry

The Quarry was once operated by an unknown construction company, who operated an advanced terraforming machine to dig into the Quarry. Today, the Terraformer is just another ruined piece of old world memorabilia, abandoned and doomed to rust in the pit it dug itself into, and the area is mainly only used as a site for prison mining by the various factions of the region.

Southpoint Village

Southpoint State is a loosely connected conglomerate of mutant tribes and communities, with it's head being the creatively named Southpoint Village, which harbors various sorts of peoples, from mutants who have been wronged by humanity, to the outcasts and fringes of human civilization. Southpoint Village has been locked in war with Bargetown since its arrival on the islands.

Sunrise Omniport

The Omniport was once a shining beacon of pre-war prosperity, a place not too indifferent from an airport, where various people around the islands could board strange vehicles, that had the means to transport them across the Lakes by air. As of today, it is another rotting memorial of the Old World.

Westside Farmhouse

A spanning farm estate, the Westside Farmhouse or Farm Estate contains a large farming site which can still be used today and an abandoned home, which can serve as a shelter if you're in a pinch.
